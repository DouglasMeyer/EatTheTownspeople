Eat the Townspeople

You are a monster in a medieval town, so what do you do? Eat and scare the townspeople.

Townspeople will flee when they see a monster, but if they form a mob they can kill it. Try to pick them off one at a time. If you get surrounded, you can roar to scare them off; but everyone will know where you are.
